Conditions

If anyone becomes a partner, it will be a mess...  So we made a condition!

  • Applicant must be the team leader
  • There must be a way to secure contact with the team
  • There must be no controversy about team members in the team applying
  • A responsible team
  • A team with a project to show off
  • Other conditions
